# 🎮 Progress RPG

**Progress RPG helps people — especially those with ADHD — start and finish real-world tasks with structured support.**

It’s not a traditional productivity app.
It’s designed to reduce friction at the exact moments people get stuck: starting, continuing, and completing things.

---

## 🧭 What it does

Progress RPG provides:

* Guided task starts (with timers and structure)
* Support flows when you get stuck
* Activity tracking and visible progress
* Systems designed to help you *follow through*, not just plan

The focus is simple:

> Help you actually do the thing — not just think about it.

---

## ⚡️ Why it exists

A lot of tools assume:

* You can start easily
* You’ll follow through
* More features = better outcomes

That’s often not true — especially for people with ADHD.

Progress RPG is built around a different idea:

> Progress comes from support at the right moment, not more complexity.
