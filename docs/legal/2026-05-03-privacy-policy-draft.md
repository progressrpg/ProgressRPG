## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Making Progress CIC ("Progress RPG", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use ProgressRPG.com, the Progress RPG app, and related products or services (the "Services").

Making Progress CIC is the data controller for personal information processed through the Services. This means we decide why and how personal information is used.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at support@progressrpg.com.

## 1. What Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect:

- Contact details, such as your email address if you register, join a waitlist, subscribe, or contact us.
- Account information, such as your username, password, account settings, login state, and subscription status.
- Profile and gameplay information, such as player names, character details, progress records, tasks, projects, activities, timers, rewards, and preferences.
- Payment and subscription information, such as payment status, plan, billing period, and transaction references. Payments are processed by third-party payment providers; we do not store full card details.
- Support and feedback information, such as messages you send us, bug reports, feature requests, and survey responses.
- Technical and usage data, such as IP address, browser type, device information, log data, pages viewed, approximate location derived from technical data, and how you use the Services.
- Cookie and analytics information, where cookies or similar technologies are used.
- Communications preferences, such as whether you have opted in or out of certain emails.

Please avoid entering sensitive personal information into free-text fields unless it is necessary.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Services.
- Create, authenticate, and manage user accounts.
- Track activity, tasks, progress, rewards, subscriptions, and related app features.
- Process payments and manage subscriptions.
- Respond to support requests, feedback, and enquiries.
- Send service messages, security notices, and important updates.
- Understand how the Services are used and improve reliability, accessibility, and user experience.
- Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, security incidents, or breaches of our Terms.
- Comply with legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations.

We do not use your personal information for profiling or solely automated decisions that have legal or similarly significant effects.

## 3. Lawful Bases for Processing

Under UK data protection law, we need a lawful basis to use personal information. Depending on the context, we rely on:

- Contract: to provide accounts, subscriptions, purchases, and core Services you request.
- Legitimate interests: to secure, maintain, debug, and improve the Services; prevent abuse; respond to support requests; and understand usage in a proportionate way.
- Consent: where required for optional cookies, analytics, marketing, or other optional processing.
- Legal obligation: where we must keep records, respond to lawful requests, comply with tax/accounting duties, or meet other legal requirements.
- Vital interests or public task: only in rare circumstances where legally relevant, such as responding to urgent safety concerns.

Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw consent at any time.

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share personal information:

- With service providers who help us run the Services, such as hosting, database, email, analytics, authentication, payment, error monitoring, and customer support providers.
- With payment providers as needed to process payments, subscriptions, fraud checks, refunds, and billing records.
- With professional advisers, insurers, auditors, or legal advisers where needed.
- With law enforcement, regulators, courts, or public authorities where required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, or security.
- If the Services, assets, or organisation are reorganised, merged, transferred, or otherwise changed, provided appropriate safeguards are used.

Service providers must only process personal information as needed to provide services to us, unless they act as independent controllers for parts of their service.

## 5. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in the United Kingdom, the European Economic Area, and other countries where we or our service providers operate.

Where personal information is transferred internationally and safeguards are required, we use appropriate safeguards such as adequacy regulations, international data transfer agreements, standard contractual clauses, or equivalent protections.

## 6. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep you signed in.
- Remember preferences.
- Protect the Services from abuse.
- Understand usage and improve the Services.
- Support analytics or performance monitoring.

Where required, we will ask for consent before using optional cookies or analytics. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

## 7. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

These measures may include access controls, encryption in transit, secure hosting, logging, backups, least-privilege access, and monitoring.

No online service can be completely secure. Please keep your password safe and contact us if you believe your account has been compromised.

## 8. How Long We Keep Your Information

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Retention periods depend on the type of information and why it is used. For example:

- Account information is usually kept while your account remains active.
- Activity, task, project, and progress records are kept while needed to provide the Services, unless you delete them or close your account.
- Payment and billing records may be kept for legal, tax, accounting, and fraud-prevention purposes.
- Support messages may be kept while needed to respond, improve support, and keep records of issues.
- Security logs may be kept for a limited period to detect and investigate abuse or security incidents.

If you close your account, we will delete or anonymise personal information unless we need to keep it for legal, security, accounting, dispute, or legitimate business reasons.

## 9. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
- Ask us to delete your personal information.
- Object to or restrict certain processing.
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
- Ask for a copy of certain information in a portable format.
- Complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

To exercise your rights, contact support@progressrpg.com.

You can also complain to the ICO if you are unhappy with how we handle your personal information. The ICO website is https://ico.org.uk.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Services are intended for users aged 18 or over.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child, contact us so we can investigate and remove it where appropriate.

## 11. Marketing Communications

If we send marketing emails, we will do so where we have a lawful basis. You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link where available or by contacting support@progressrpg.com.

We may still send service, security, account, billing, or legal messages where needed.

## 12. Third-Party Links and Services

The Services may link to third-party websites or services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy notices.

We are not responsible for third-party websites or services that we do not control.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make significant changes, we will notify you by email, through the Services, or by another reasonable method. The updated policy takes effect when posted unless we state a later date.
